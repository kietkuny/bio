EXPAND YOUR WORKSPACE

Utilize our Multi Monitor App for seamless Screen Casting to television, tablet, or smartphone. Perfect for professionals who need an Extension Screen or an additional Display Monitor while traveling. Compatible with USB and LAN connections.

ULTIMATE REMOTE ACCESS AND CONTROL

Experience comprehensive control with spacedesk, offering Remote Access and Control over USB, WiFi, and LAN. Turn your device into an Input Console or a Virtual Monitor for your Desktop PC.

WIRELESS DISPLAY SOLUTIONS

Connect wirelessly with our Wireless Display Monitor, similar to Miracast, RDP, AirPlay, and Sidecar. Ideal for Screen Mirroring over the air or via cable, enhancing your connectivity on the go.

CREATIVE AND PRODUCTIVE

Transform your Windows tablet into a sketchbook for drawing artwork, or use it as a Creative Video Wall App with any angle rotation. spacedesk facilitates your artistic and professional displays effortlessly.

ENHANCED DESKTOP STREAMING

Stream your desktop workspace with audio through our Windows Desktop Streaming feature. Perfect for educators and presenters using our Projector Screen Viewer or those needing a Windows Graphics Tablet App.

POWERFUL DISPLAY EXTENSIONS

Extend or duplicate your Windows Desktop Workspace with our App. Use it as a Portable Multi-monitor Laptop Screen, perfect for extending your visual workspace or duplicating it as needed.

TRAVEL WITH TECHNOLOGY

Access your main computer from your mobile device, turning any TV, mobile, or tablet screen into a Second Display On The Go. Ideal for travelers who need a Portable Multimonitor solution.

INPUT AND OUTPUT SOLUTIONS

spacedesk offers a comprehensive Software Display Hub and Software Display Switch solution, making any tablet an Input Terminal or a powerful Input Device.

VERSATILE KVM INTEGRATION

Our Software KVM-Switch allows you to manage multiple devices with a single keyboard, video, and mouse setup, simplifying your hardware needs and boosting productivity.

PROJECT ANYWHERE

Use our App as a Projector Screen Viewer, ideal for presentations and large-scale displays. It supports Windows Desktop Duplication and is an alternative to traditional projection systems.
